DocVault 🛡️ - Your Ultimate Offline Document Safe

DocVault is a high-security, offline-first document vault designed for users who prioritize privacy. Keep your sensitive documents—such as IDs, contracts, passports, and medical records—encrypted and accessible only to you, with absolutely zero cloud dependency. 

Your data belongs to you. No accounts. No subscriptions. No data mining. 

Key Features:

🔒 Military-Grade Encryption
All files are encrypted locally on your device using industry-standard AES-GCM encryption. Your data is locked down and unreadable to anyone but you.

📱 Biometric Authentication & Secure PIN
Access your vault instantly and securely using Fingerprint or Face ID. A highly secure, mathematically hashed PIN system serves as your unbreakable fallback.

📂 100% Offline-First Privacy
DocVault does not have servers, and your data never leaves your device. Everything is processed and stored locally for total privacy by design.

📑 Organize with Ease
Attach multiple pages or related files to a single document entry. Sort your files using custom tags and categories so you can always find what you need instantly.

🕒 Smart Expiry Reminders
Never miss a renewal date again. Set expiry dates on important documents (like passports or insurance policies) and receive local notifications before they expire.

🌓 Beautiful Adaptive UI
Enjoy a clean, modern interface that respects your system's dark and light modes, making document management a visually pleasing experience.

Security Notice:
DocVault takes your security seriously. Because your data is encrypted offline, we have no way to recover your files if you lose your PIN and biometric access is unavailable. Please ensure you remember your PIN!
